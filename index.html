About Will Bohmann

At the core, I am a pure constructivist. I believe that new learning builds on prior knowledge. We remember that which we experience, the events we make connections to and are engaged in. Real learning is not just in the telling and doing. Deep learning comes from recalling and reflecting. I believe we make meaning from the environment around us. It is imperative that educators set up that environment for success to facilitate deep connections.

I've been working in the field of education for over twenty five years. If there is one constant it is that people are well adapted to learning. The role of the teacher is to help students reapze their full potential as a learner.

When I'm not working as an educator, I like to get outdoors. Vermont is a great place to live in all seasons. Favorite activities include running the backroads, hiking in the Green Mountains and biking the trail networks near my house. I also enjoy cooking and hanging out with my family.
